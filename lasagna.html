  • 2 teaspoons Spectrum Naturals Olive Oil
  • 2 (24 ounce) jars pasta sauce
  • 1 (340 g) package Yves Veggie Cuisine Italian Veggie Ground Round
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
